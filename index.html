What is WMSU-Archive?

The WMSU-Archives is the official digital platform for preserving, showcasing, and sharing the scholarly work of Western Mindanao State University. This centralized repository brings together journals from across disciplines, making research more visible, searchable, and accessible to students, educators, and researchers both locally and globally.

Explore resources, submit your work, and stay informed.

The Disputation of America's Moral Authority in Nuremberg (sample chapter)

(2025-03-30) Miller, Dawn

The Disputation of America's Moral Authority in Nuremberg constitutes a chapter in the monograph entitled Service in the Shadow of Justice: The Legacy of the Black American Military Court Guards in the Nuremberg Military Tribunals. The full work demonstrates that the assignment of Black troops to security details at the Nuremberg Military Tribunals was not merely a staffing decision but a complex interplay of racial politics, military strategy, and America's attempt to project moral authority in post-war Europe.
